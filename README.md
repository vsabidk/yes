## ğŸŒ What Is the Environment?

The environment encompasses all living and non-living components that surround organisms. It includes:

- **Natural elements:** air, water, soil, climate
- **Living organisms:** plants, animals, microorganisms
- **Human-made systems:** cities, infrastructure, industries

Healthy environments support life, regulate climate, and provide essential resources.

- Supports all life forms
- Provides food, water, and clean air

Without a healthy environment, human survival and societal stability are at risk.

## âš ï¸ Major Environmental Challenges

### 1. Climate Change

Caused primarily by greenhouse gas emissions from fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes.

**Impacts include:**

- Rising global temperatures
- Extreme weather events
- Melting ice caps and sea-level rise

---

### 2. Pollution

Pollution affects air, water, and soil quality.

- **Air Pollution:** Emissions from vehicles and industries
- **Water Pollution:** Plastic waste, chemicals, oil spills
- **Soil Pollution:** Pesticides, heavy metals, landfills

---

### 3. Deforestation

Forests are being cleared for agriculture, logging, and urban development, leading to habitat loss and increased carbon emissions.

---

### 4. Biodiversity Loss

Species extinction rates are accelerating due to habitat destruction, climate change, and overexploitation.
